IGNIS Robotics at Milpitas High School

Training the Next Generation of Engineers

We are an FIRST Robotics Competition team of students who combine engineering, creativity, and teamwork to compete in robotics and bring STEM to our community.

Our team uses FIRST Robotics Competition as a platform to inspire and educate students in STEM. Most of our members join with little to no robotics experience, and we proudly welcome students of all skill levels. Through hands-on learning and mentorship, we empower each member to develop technical skills, confidence, and a passion for STEM. We create an environment where any student—regardless of background—can explore and pursue opportunities in STEM.

Learn more about our team

Student Led

Our team is entirely student-led, with members taking on key roles in all aspects of our projects. This allows students to gain team working experience and develop leadership skills while pursuing their passion for STEM.
