Why Sponsor Us?

By sponsoring IGNIS Robotics, you are investing in the future of STEM education and innovation. Your support helps us build competitive robots, develop leadership skills, and inspire the next generation of engineers and innovators. Together, we can make a lasting impact on our community and beyond.

  • 83% of FIRST alumni pursue STEM majors by year four of college.
  • 51% of female FIRST alumni have a major in engineering or CS by fourth year of college.
  • 63% of FIRST alumni are currently employed in a STEM field.

Building and competing in FIRST Robotics is an expensive and resource-intensive process. Your sponsorship helps cover critical costs such as robot parts, motors, electronics, fabrication materials, specialized tools, and registration fees for competitions. It also supports travel, equipment maintenance, and access to the technology students need to design, test, and refine high-performance robots.

By funding IGNIS Robotics, you’re not just helping students build robots—you’re giving them the resources to gain hands-on engineering experience, practice teamwork and project management, and develop problem-solving skills that prepare them for college and careers in STEM. Every dollar directly translates into opportunities for innovation, growth, and real-world learning.

Donation Processing

Our team is an official organization of MHS. All donations must be processed through our school, MHS, which is part of the Milpitas Unified School District (MUSD).

Milpitas Unified School District is a government organization with IRS status 170(c)(1). Donations made to our team through the district may be tax-deductible.
